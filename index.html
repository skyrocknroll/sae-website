Frequently Asked Questions

What types of foundry equipment does Sree Abirami manufacture?

We manufacture a comprehensive range of foundry equipment including shot blasting machines (hanger type, tumblast, simple), sand preparation systems (intensive mixers, sand dryers, sand coolers), material handling equipment (vibratory shakeouts, pneumatic conveyors), molten metal systems (ladles, ladle preheaters), and various foundry accessories like dust collectors and pedestal grinders.

How long has Sree Abirami been manufacturing foundry equipment?

Sree Abirami Equipments has been a trusted manufacturer of foundry equipment since the 1980s, bringing over 40 years of experience and expertise to the foundry industry. Our decades of experience have helped us understand the evolving needs of foundries and develop innovative solutions.

What is the capacity range of your shot blasting machines?

Our shot blasting machines are available in various capacities to suit different foundry requirements. We offer hanger type shot blasting machines for heavy castings, tumblast machines for batch processing, and simple shot blasting systems for smaller operations. Capacities range from small batch systems to high-volume continuous operation units, customized based on your specific casting sizes and production requirements.

Do you provide installation and maintenance services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive installation and maintenance services. Our expert technicians handle professional installation, commissioning, and testing of all equipment. We also offer regular maintenance programs, spare parts supply, 24/7 technical support, and operator training to ensure optimal equipment performance and longevity.

What is the typical delivery time for foundry equipment?

Delivery times vary depending on the equipment type and customization requirements. Standard equipment typically takes 4-8 weeks, while custom-designed solutions may require 8-12 weeks. We work closely with our clients to meet their project timelines and provide regular updates throughout the manufacturing process.

Can you customize equipment based on specific requirements?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom manufacturing and can design equipment tailored to your specific foundry requirements. Our engineering team works closely with clients to understand their unique needs, space constraints, production volumes, and operational preferences to develop optimal solutions that maximize efficiency and productivity.

What quality certifications do your products have?

All our products undergo stringent quality control processes and comprehensive testing procedures. We maintain the highest manufacturing standards with advanced CAD systems, simulation testing, and quality assurance protocols. Our equipment is built to meet international quality standards and comes with performance guarantees.

Which regions do you serve for foundry equipment supply?

We primarily serve customers across Tamil Nadu, South India, and other parts of India. Based in Coimbatore, we are strategically located to serve the industrial belt of Tamil Nadu and have successfully supplied equipment to foundries across various regions. We also provide support for international inquiries.

What after-sales support do you provide?

Our comprehensive after-sales support includes regular maintenance services, genuine spare parts supply, technical troubleshooting, operator training programs, equipment upgrades, and 24/7 customer support. We believe in building long-term partnerships and ensuring optimal equipment performance throughout its lifecycle.

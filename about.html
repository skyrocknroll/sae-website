Our Journey

Sree Abirami Equipments was established in the 1980s in Karamadai, Coimbatore, South India, with a vision to provide world-class foundry equipment and solutions. Over the decades, we have grown from a small manufacturing unit to a leading name in the foundry industry.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and a deep understanding of our customers' evolving needs. We have successfully delivered foundry solutions that have helped our clients achieve operational excellence and competitive advantage.

  • Established in the 1980s
  • Based in Karamadai, Coimbatore
  • Team of 40+ skilled professionals
  • Decades of industry experience
  • Long-lasting customer relationships
  • Serving customers across India
